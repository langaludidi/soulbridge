About Tandiwe

In loving memory of Tandiwe Olga "MaFaku" Tini — a cherished mother, grandmother, and guiding light to all who knew her. Her warmth, wisdom, and unwavering spirit will live on in our hearts forever.

Tandiwe was born on 13 July 1955 in eMagcakini Village, Libode, to the late MaHadi Mbeki and her late husband. After the passing of her father, her mother returned with the children to her own village, where Tandiwe was raised in the warmth of her extended family.

Life Story

Tandiwe was born on 13 July 1955 in eMagcakini Village, Libode, to the late MaHadi Mbeki and her late husband. After the passing of her father, her mother returned with the children to her own village, where Tandiwe was raised in the warmth of her extended family.

Early Life and Education

Growing up in the Eastern Cape, Tandiwe learned the values of community, respect, and hard work from an early age. Her education was a priority, and she excelled in her studies, always showing a keen interest in helping others and serving her community.

Family and Marriage

Tandiwe married her beloved husband Tshawe "Bro Mxo" Tini, and together they built a loving family. She was a devoted mother to her sons Maki and Kulile, instilling in them the same values of love, respect, and service that had guided her throughout her life.

Legacy of Love

Tandiwe's final message to the Ludidi family was filled with gratitude and love: "To the Ludidi family ndiyabulela kakhulu bakhozi bam. nindomelezile ngala msg, ethembeni kuzoba rite bakhozi bam." Her words continue to comfort those she left behind.
