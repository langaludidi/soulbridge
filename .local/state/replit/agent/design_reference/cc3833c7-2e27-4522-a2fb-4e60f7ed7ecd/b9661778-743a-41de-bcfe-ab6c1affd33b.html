About Tandiwe

In loving memory of Tandiwe Olga "MaFaku" Tini — a cherished mother, grandmother, and guiding light to all who knew her. Her warmth, wisdom, and unwavering spirit will live on in our hearts forever.

Tandiwe was born on 13 July 1955 in eMagcakini Village, Libode, to the late MaHadi Mbeki and her late husband. After the passing of her father, her mother returned with the children to her own village, where Tandiwe was raised in the warmth of community and tradition.
