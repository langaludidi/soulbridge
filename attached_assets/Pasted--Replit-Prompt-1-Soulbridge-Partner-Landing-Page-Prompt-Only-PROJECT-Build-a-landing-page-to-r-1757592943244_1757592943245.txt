✅ Replit Prompt 1: Soulbridge Partner Landing Page (Prompt Only)

PROJECT: Build a landing page to recruit and convert Soulbridge partners — including funeral homes, churches, NGOs, municipalities, and family-owned businesses.

GOAL:
Create a clean, emotionally resonant, and trust-building page that introduces the Soulbridge partnership models and leads users to sign up, book a call, or request a demo.

PRIMARY AUDIENCE:

Funeral home owners

Faith leaders and church administrators

Community organisations

Memorial service providers

Local government teams

STRUCTURE:

1. Hero Section

Headline: “Bring Dignified Digital Memorials to Your Community”

Subheadline: “Partner with Soulbridge to offer online tributes and funeral programs under your name or brand.”

CTA: “Become a Partner” / “Request Info Pack” / “Book a Call”

2. How It Works

Icons or steps:

Sign up

Choose branding level (co-brand, white-label, referral)

Start publishing digital memorials

Earn revenue and support your families

3. Partner Options Overview

Three cards:

🤝 Co-Branded

🔖 White-Label

📣 Referral

Each includes: what’s included, pricing summary, and ideal use case

4. Testimonials or Scenarios

Example: “Mokoena Funerals now includes Soulbridge in every package — no more printing stress, just a link to honour the departed.”

5. Why Partner With Soulbridge?

Emotional and operational benefits

Reduce costs for families

Promote your brand

Generate recurring revenue

Support dignified remembrance

6. CTA Section

“Start in under 5 minutes”

Button: “Apply Now” or “Talk to Our Team”

7. FAQ

Pricing, support, setup, billing, how to cancel, white-label options

8. Footer

Contact info, social links, legal disclaimer

Note: "Soulbridge is proudly built for South African communities"

DESIGN GOALS:

Warm, calming, and trustworthy look

Use neutral greys, deep blue, soft icons

Simple, clear typography

Optimized for mobile (funeral home staff may be on phones)

✅ Replit Prompt 2: Soulbridge Partner User Journey

PROJECT: Define the full user journey for a Soulbridge partner — from landing page visit to full deployment of digital memorials.

GOAL:
Map a respectful, smooth partner onboarding experience that supports different models (co-brand, white-label, referral), and creates long-term engagement.

1. Discovery

User sees a social post, email, or hears from a Soulbridge rep

Clicks to the partner landing page

2. Interest

Reads about the 3 partner models

Watches short demo or testimonial

Downloads info pack or compares packages

3. Action

Fills out a brief form: name, business name, location, email

Selects interest: Co-branded, White-label, or Referral

Gets email confirmation and follow-up message

4. Onboarding

Receives welcome email + branding guide

Co-brand: Uploads logo, contact info, intro text

White-label: Submits domain or subdomain info

Referral: Gets unique link and pre-written messages to share

5. Activation

Accesses dashboard

Creates first test memorial

Sees how branding appears in public link or PDF

Gets optional support via chat, email, or Zoom

6. Real Use

Begins adding client memorials

Shares links with families

Collects revenue (if set up for share)

7. Growth

Receives monthly email with usage and earnings

Optional upgrades: vanity links, printed PDFs, domain changes

Stays engaged via success stories or product updates

KEY MILESTONES:

Form submitted

First branded memorial created

First public share

First referral/commission or client payment

Long-term: 10+ memorials or upgrade to white-label
