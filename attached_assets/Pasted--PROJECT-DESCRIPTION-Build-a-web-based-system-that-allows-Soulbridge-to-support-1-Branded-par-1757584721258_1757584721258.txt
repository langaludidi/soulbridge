
⸻

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
Build a web-based system that allows Soulbridge to support:
	1.	Branded partnerships with funeral homes, churches, NGOs, and cultural groups
	2.	White-label licensing for large institutions wanting to run Soulbridge under their own domain and brand
	3.	Referral partners who earn revenue by directing users to Soulbridge

⸻

OBJECTIVES:
	•	Extend Soulbridge’s reach and trust through local partners
	•	Enable recurring revenue through partner packages
	•	Allow partners to provide digital memorials to their own clients
	•	Offer clear branding and reporting for each partner type
	•	Allow both one-time and subscription pricing options
	•	Maintain Soulbridge’s standards of dignity, clarity, and simplicity across all implementations

⸻

THREE PARTNERSHIP MODELS:

⸻

1. Co-Branded Partner
	•	Partner uses Soulbridge’s main platform
	•	Their logo appears alongside Soulbridge on memorial pages
	•	They receive their own login to manage memorials
	•	Revenue is shared for each paying client brought in

Pricing suggestions:
	•	Free onboarding
	•	Soulbridge retains 70% of revenue per memorial, partner earns 30%
	•	Optional R99/month fee for analytics and branded dashboard access

⸻

2. White-Label License
	•	Partner receives their own branded domain or subdomain
	•	All branding, headers, footers, and PDFs reflect their name
	•	Soulbridge provides technical maintenance and support
	•	Ideal for funeral chains, insurers, municipalities, or faith networks

Pricing suggestions:
	•	Setup fee: R2,500 once-off
	•	Hosting: R499–R999/month
	•	Custom domain support: optional
	•	Unlimited memorials included or capped at negotiated amount
	•	SLA-based support (response within 24 hrs)

⸻

3. Referral Partner
	•	No dashboard required
	•	Partner shares a unique referral link or code
	•	When someone purchases a paid plan, the partner earns a commission

Pricing suggestions:
	•	R50–R100 per successful referral
	•	Monthly payment based on referral total
	•	Optional digital toolkit provided (WhatsApp template, QR posters, etc.)

⸻

KEY FEATURES TO SUPPORT:
	•	Partner dashboards (co-brand/white-label)
	•	Login for partner admins
	•	Branded public memorial display pages
	•	PDF output branded per partner
	•	Referral code tracking and reporting
	•	Partner-level billing and reporting tools
	•	Access control (partners only see their own content)
	•	Graceful onboarding with optional Soulbridge support

⸻

GOALS FOR USER EXPERIENCE:
	•	Easy for partners to get started
	•	Branding should appear dignified and respectful
	•	Partner value is visible immediately (e.g., their logo on a memorial page)
	•	Dashboard and PDF views must maintain emotional sensitivity

⸻

USE CASE EXAMPLES:
	•	A local funeral home creates memorials for clients as part of their funeral packages
	•	A municipality offers memorials for public servants and local heroes
	•	A church provides a digital Book of Remembrance for its members
	•	An NGO offers grief support through free digital tributes and upgrades

⸻

REVENUE MODEL OPTIONS (Soulbridge):
	•	Recurring monthly revenue from white-label licenses
	•	Revenue share from co-branded partners
	•	Passive income from referral commissions
	•	Tiered pricing to support both small and large partners

⸻

DELIVERABLES EXPECTED FROM THE DEVELOPER:
	•	Admin interface for Soulbridge to manage all partners
	•	Partner interfaces with branding, dashboard, and access control
	•	Mechanism to track referral codes or partner-attributed signups
	•	Flexible pricing structure with ability to adjust features by partner type
	•	Printable PDFs and public pages customized per partner
