👥 Parties Involved in Soulbridge
1. 🧍‍♂️🧍‍♀️ Public Users (Families, Friends, Communities)

Who they are:

Everyday people visiting the site to view or create memorials

Bereaved families wanting to honour a loved one

Friends or community members leaving messages, tributes, or flowers

What they can do:

View public memorials, programs, events, and partner listings

Submit new funeral programs or memorials via forms (if logged in)

Leave guestbook entries or tributes

Download or print programs or obituaries

Engage based on subscription tier (e.g., view galleries if premium)

2. 📝 Submitters / Contributors

Who they are:

Logged-in users (usually family members or organizers)

Can be limited to a Contributor role in WordPress

What they can do:

Submit new:

Funeral programs

Memorials

Memorial events

(In testing) Partner listings

Their posts are saved as drafts for admin moderation

May receive email updates or confirmations

3. 👩‍💼 Administrators (Site Moderators & Managers)

Who they are:

Soulbridge team members

Site owners or trusted moderators

What they can do:

Full access to all content (CPTs, ACF fields, Breakdance templates)

Approve or edit submitted posts

Manage partner accounts and content visibility

Customize forms, layouts, styles

Manage subscription levels manually (or via WooCommerce in future)

Special responsibilities:

Content moderation (respect, dignity, no spam)

Ensuring cultural sensitivity and data privacy

Approving partner listings and events

Maintaining data backups and security

4. 🤝 Partner Organizations

Who they are:

Funeral homes

Cultural organizations

Florists

Caterers

Musicians

Memorial photographers

Faith leaders or spiritual advisors

What they can do:

Create and manage a Partner profile

Submit via form or through admin support

Appear in the Partner Directory with:

Type (e.g. Funeral Home)

Province & Address

Website & Social media

Logo and Description

Future opportunities:

Upgrade visibility via paid tiers (Professional / Enterprise)

Appear in related memorials or events

Respond to family requests
